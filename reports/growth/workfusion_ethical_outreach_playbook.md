# Workfusion Ethical Outreach Playbook

## Positioning

Workfusion is an AI EA Generator + Debugger for MT4/MT5 traders. The promise is software workflow improvement:

- generate complete MQL drafts from strategy ideas,
- debug compiler errors,
- run risk/readiness checks,
- organize projects,
- download MQL outputs,
- compile through MetaEditor when the worker is online.

Do not pitch guaranteed profit, funded account passing, signals, or investment returns.

## Hard Rules

- Do not buy email lists.
- Do not scrape private emails.
- Do not mass-message people who did not opt in.
- Do not imply Workfusion trades for the user.
- Do not ask for broker credentials, API keys, or trading account access.
- Do not promise compile success for arbitrary code without review.
- Do not promise profitable backtests.

## Allowed Acquisition Motions

1. SEO resource pages.
   Publish useful pages for exact MQL problems and route visitors into opt-in forms.

2. Helpful community answers.
   Answer the technical question first. Link only when the Workfusion guide directly helps.

3. Opt-in email.
   Send only to people who joined through the form or directly asked for updates.

4. Founder-led LinkedIn posts.
   Show product workflow, screenshots, limitations, and specific debugging lessons.

5. Partner and affiliate conversations.
   Talk to MQL freelancers, EA sellers, prop trading educators, and automation communities with written boundaries.

## Daily 45-Minute Routine

1. Check `/growth` for new leads and support issues.
2. Reply to open support first.
3. Pick one guide from `/resources`.
4. Find one relevant community discussion where the answer is genuinely useful.
5. Post one short LinkedIn build note or debugging lesson.
6. Move contacted leads to the correct CRM stage.
7. Record what produced replies, not just views.

## Lead Stages

- `new`: opted in, not reviewed.
- `researching`: likely fit, needs context.
- `contacted`: a human reply was sent.
- `trial`: using free workflow or testing.
- `customer`: converted to paid.
- `nurture`: not ready, keep useful updates only.
- `closed`: not a fit or opted out.

## First Conversion Target

The first goal is not 10k cold emails. The first goal is 10 serious builders who:

- tried the generator/debugger,
- produced a real MQL draft,
- reported a bug or limitation,
- understand it is a development tool,
- are willing to pay when it saves time.
